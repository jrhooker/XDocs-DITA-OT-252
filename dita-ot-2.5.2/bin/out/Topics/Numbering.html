Numbering Issues to Note

  1. Prefaces should be unnumbered in the body and the TOC.

  2. Topic Level Numbering should be define-able.

  3. Appendix numbering is it letters

  4. Page numbering should restart at the beginning of the first chapter.

  5. Frontmatter numbering is in Roman numerals.

  6. Revision tables should not show up in the list of tables or the total count of tables.
